# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account

**How do I reset my password?**
Go to the login page and click "Forgot password." Enter your email address
and you will receive a reset link within 5 minutes. The link expires after
24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact
support.

**How do I update my email address?**
Navigate to Settings → Account → Contact Information. Enter your new email
address and click Save. A confirmation email will be sent to the new address.
You must confirm it before the change takes effect.

**Can I have multiple users on one account?**
Yes. Enterprise and Team plans support multiple seats. Go to Settings →
Team to invite members. Each member gets their own login credentials.

## Billing

**What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. All payments are
processed securely via Stripe.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until
the end of the current billing period. We do not offer prorated refunds for
mid-cycle cancellations.

**Do you offer refunds?**
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. After 14 days,
refunds are not available except in cases of billing errors. Contact
support@resolveai.com to request a refund.
