## FAQ

**Can I switch plans?**
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect
immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Is there a free trial for paid plans?**
All new accounts start on the Starter plan. Pro plans include a 14-day
free trial with no credit card required.
