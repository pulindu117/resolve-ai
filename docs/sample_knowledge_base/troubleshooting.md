# Troubleshooting Guide

## Login Issues

**I can't log in — it says my password is incorrect.**
1. Ensure Caps Lock is off.
2. Try resetting your password via the "Forgot password" link.
3. If the problem persists, your account may be locked after 5 failed
   attempts. Wait 30 minutes or contact support.

**I'm not receiving the password reset email.**
- Check your spam or junk folder.
- Make sure you're entering the email address associated with your account.
- Allow up to 10 minutes for delivery during high-traffic periods.
- If still not received, contact support@resolveai.com.

## Payment Failures

**My payment was declined.**
Common reasons for payment failure:
- Insufficient funds
- Card expired
- Billing address mismatch
- Bank flagged the transaction as suspicious

Try a different payment method or contact your bank. If your account is
suspended due to a failed payment, it will be reactivated automatically
within 24 hours of a successful payment.

## Performance

**The app is slow to respond.**
Response times depend on the size of your knowledge base and query
complexity. If responses consistently take more than 10 seconds, try:
1. Reducing the number of indexed documents
2. Using more specific queries
3. Contacting support if the issue persists
